You are not alone.

We provide support for and raise awareness of partners and family members of Tech Facilitated Sexual Violence offenders in Singapore.

Partners and family members of sexual offenders are oft-overlooked innocent secondary victims harmed by the sex offence. We seek to reduce the stigma of having loved a sexual offender, and to support those who find themselves in this situation.

Resources

Counselling

Need confidential help? We can connect you with trained counsellors, including child and marital counsellors.

Support group

We provide virtual and in-person confidential support groups. Entry to our support groups is strictly for vetted invitees only. Apply online now.

Legal

If you wish to understand your legal rights, we can connect you to legal clinics for a one-time consultation.
